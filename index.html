About the Event

Welcome to the UIU CSE Fest registration portal. This exciting event brings together students, professionals, and enthusiasts in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. Before you begin the registration process, please take a moment to review the important information below.

Team Requirements

  • Each team must consist of 3-4 members, including the team leader.
  • All team members must be currently enrolled students at their respective universities.
  • Teams must provide valid student ID cards and recent photographs of all members during registration.

Registration Process

  1. Team and Team Leader's Information: Provide team name and team leader's details.
  2. Team Members Information: Add information for all team members, including their university ID cards and photographs.
  3. Payment: Complete the registration by making the payment. Multiple payment methods are available.

Important Notes

  • Please ensure all information provided is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Keep your transaction ID safe after making the payment.
  • Team members cannot be changed after registration is complete.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable.
